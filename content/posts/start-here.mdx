This site is my place to record what I am learning. It should stay simple enough that writing remains easy, but structured enough that old notes are easy to find.

## Writing flow

Each note lives in `content/posts` as an MDX file. A post needs frontmatter:

```mdx
---
title: "Post title"
description: "One sentence summary."
date: "2026-05-15"
category: "Web Development"
tags: ["Next.js", "TypeScript"]
---
```

After that, the note is normal Markdown plus MDX. Headings become readable sections, code blocks stay formatted, and tags connect related notes.

## What belongs here

- Concepts I want to remember
- Small tutorials I have completed
- Debugging notes from real projects
- Summaries of books, courses, and documentation
- Questions I want to revisit later

## Next improvement ideas

The MVP uses local MDX files and client-side search. Later versions could add a graph view, backlinks, draft mode, or a richer editor.
